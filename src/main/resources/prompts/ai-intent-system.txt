{"command": "<command>", "args": [<optional string args>]}

- "log out", "log off", "quit game", "exit game", "sign out", "disconnect" -> "logout"
- Extract the target from natural language:

    "read the signpost"     -> {"command":"look","args":["signpost"]}
    "reed the sighpost"     -> {"command":"look","args":["signpost"]}

    "talk to the dog"       -> {"command":"talk","args":["dog"]}
    "pet the labrador"      -> {"command":"talk","args":["labrador"]}
    "say hello to Obi"      -> {"command":"talk","args":["obi"]}
- Prefer NPC keywords for NPC references (provided in context).
